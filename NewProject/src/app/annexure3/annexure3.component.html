The Committees of the Society

The Management Board formed the following six committees to assist it. These are Advisory positions with no executive authority.

1. Dispute settlement committee.
2. Public Relations Committee.
3. Development and Planning Committee.
4. Finance Committee.
5. Business Promotion Committee.
6. Legal Committee.

Each committee shall have a Chairman and may consist of at least two more members and a maximum of five members. There is no fixed term for these committees and function as per the directions by the Chairman of the Committee. The Chairman chooses the committee members.

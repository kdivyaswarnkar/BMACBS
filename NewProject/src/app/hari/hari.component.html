Details of G. Hari Gopala Rao (President)

SHRI. G. HARI GOPALA RAO

FROM 25-04-2022 TO 24-04-2027

Born on 12th August, 1950 in Bobbili, Vizianagaram District to Shri. G. Venkatarao and Smt. G. Appayyamma. He studied BSc.

He worked in Police Department from the ending of S.I of Police to Additional S.P. He received 2 Mementos Service, Above 40 Cash Rewards, and Above 25 Good Service certificates.

Married to G. Lakshmi Chellayyamma. Has two daughters, both well educated. One daughter working as a HOD (computer science) in Pydah college, Boyapalem, Visakhapatnam. Another daughter is a housewife.

Election Council

It is an independent body exclusively concerned with the elections to the posts of Directors in each year. The present council was constituted in the year Feb 2022.

Thus, it will organize the elections to the Management Board in 2019, 2020 and 2021. Generally, the elections are held in the month of April, each year. At the time of elections,the council appoints an election officer to assist the council and attend to the election process with full authority.
